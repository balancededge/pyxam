* Solving Least Squares
Trying out plotting features:
<<fig=True>>=
x = (0:25) + randn(1, 26);
y = linspace(0, 5, length(x));
a = x' \ y'
plot(x , y, 'o')
hold on
plot(x, a*x, 'r')
hold off
figure()
hist(a*x - y)
title('Histogram of residuals')
@
And include a plot but hide the code:

x = linspace(0, 4*pi, 200);

hold on

hold off

ylabel('sinc(x)')
